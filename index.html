    Our Humble Beginning

    Our team is proposing the system that will give the real time indicator of the garbage collection in garbage bins.These smart bins will seemlessly detect its garbage content and will notify you and the municipality authority about the same.Each bin will have one ID and one registered mail of the user. There are two partitions in the bin one for biodegradable and non-biodegradabel waste.Every time when it will be full, a message will be send to the authority and the respective person.
